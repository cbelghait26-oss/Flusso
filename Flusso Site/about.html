About Flusso

Why it exists

Traditional task managers are good at collecting actions but weak at providing direction. Flusso keeps daily work connected to higher-level goals so prioritization is clearer and follow-through is more stable.

The method

  • Define objectives that set direction.
  • Link tasks to objectives to keep meaning attached.
  • Execute through structured focus sessions.
  • Track focused time and completed actions to calibrate.

Psychology

Flusso’s core mechanic is task→objective alignment, aligned with ideas discussed under Goal Systems Theory (Kruglanski): linking lower-level actions to higher-order goals can increase persistence and perceived meaning.

Vision

Long term: a full web platform expanding beyond mobile constraints — advanced planning surfaces, document workflows inside focus sessions, whiteboards/notes, deeper analytics, and a personal AI agent.
